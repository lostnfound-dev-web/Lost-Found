Welcome to
Lost & Found

This platform helps students and staff easily report, find, and verify lost items within the university. Our goal is to make lost-property management simple, fast, and transparent.

Search Lost Items

Browse all reported items by category, date, or location. Results can be filtered and verified by admins.

📤

Report Found Items

If you found something on university grounds, submit a short description and upload a photo.

✅

Verification System

Each item is verified by an administrator before being listed as “Found” or “Pending”.

About This Project

The Lost & Found platform is a group project for the Databases & Web Systems course at Constructor University. It demonstrates database integration, web design, and teamwork.
